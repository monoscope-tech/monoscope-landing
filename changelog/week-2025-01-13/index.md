This week we focused on improving the onboarding experience with better state management.

- Persist onboarding stage progress across sessions
- Users can now resume onboarding from where they left off

### Onboarding Navigation

- Improved linking between onboarding pages for smoother flow
- Better navigation structure throughout the setup process
